《I Strove with None》
— Walter Savage Landor

I strove with none,
for none was worth my strife;
Nature I lov’d, ，
and next to Nature, Art;
I warm’d both hands before the fire of life;
It sinks,
and I am ready to depart.

我和谁都不争，
和谁争我都不屑；
我爱大自然，
其次就是艺术；
我双手烤着，
生命之火取暖；
火萎了，
我也准备走了。
